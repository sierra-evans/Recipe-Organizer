Old Fashioned Pancakes

Breakfast

Quick and easy delicious, fluffy pancakes.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  1. Mix together all ingredients into a batter.
  2. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto skillet over medium-high heat. Flip after two minutes, cook for two more minutes.
